
## Login issues

You must have a work account for Google that you use for the HackerRank login.

Signing In to HackerRank Account with Google for Work Login

Navigate to the HackerRank for Work Login page. The Google for work login is displayed along with the HackerRank login.

Click Continue with Google to log in to your HackerRank account.

The Choose an account page is displayed that shows all your Google for Work accounts.

Select an account that you have already used to log in to HackerRank. It must be your work account.

If you use an account that is not a work account, then an error is displayed:

Also, if the account is not valid, an error message is displayed.
