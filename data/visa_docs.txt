

[ LOGIN ]

To log in to your credit card account, please visit your issuer or bank website. The website and freephone number (only free when dialling from within the USA) may
be located on the front or back of your Visa card. 



[ SECURITY ISSUES ]

Visit our Lost or Stolen card page – if the identity theft involves your Visa card – to learn how to cancel your card or get an emergency replacement card.




[ VERIFICATIONS ]

As a consumer, there’s nothing you need to register for 3-D Secure. Issuers (a financial institution, like a bank or a credit union) handle the authentication that
3D Secure provides. Your online transactions are secured through Visa and our enhanced security makes purchasing more seamless – while still protecting you from
fraud.
